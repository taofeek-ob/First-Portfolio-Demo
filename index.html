About

Taofeek Bakare is a 4th year medical student of Ahmadu Bello University. He has over two year experience in volunteering. He is a tech enthusiast, devising ways how innovations can bridge information divide, reduce economy inequality and promote value driven learning. Currently, Taofeek is the Editor-in-Chief at Poetry House Nigeria, where he tasks on bridging knowledge gap, curating writers and encouraging personal development. He's also a social leader through his premium personal mentorship class.

Taofeek has undergone numerous personal development courses, leadership trainings, and seminars, all which he is duly certified. Taofeek hopes, through the ripple effect, to inspire new generation of servant leaders with immense contributory drive to local and global development. He plans to effect changes in ways policies are made and implemented. He loves reading financial, philosophy and logic, psychology, and history books.

His favourite book of 2019 was Prosperity Paradox: how innovations can lift nations out of poverty. Taofeek describes himself as an autodidact, a skeptical empiricist and someone who reasons unconventionally and inversely.
